Section 02 · Global Issue

Heritage vs. Modernity

Balancing rapid modern tourism development with the preservation of irreplaceable historical sites. Students will investigate a living tension playing out on the Aegean coast.

Heritage Preservation Maritime Culture Sustainable Tourism
Where ancient ruins meet luxury marinas
2,500+
years of continuous civilization in Bodrum / Halicarnassus
  • 🏛️
    Ancient Heritage at Risk
    Students analyze the Mausoleum at Halicarnassus and Myndos Gate — the historic battleground of Alexander the Great — and study the pressures modern development places on these irreplaceable sites.
  • ⚓
    Displacement of Local Communities
    The high-end Yalıkavak Marina exemplifies how luxury infrastructure transforms traditional fishing villages — students investigate the socioeconomic ripple effects on local livelihoods.
  • 🌊
    Underwater Archaeology
    The Bodrum Museum of Underwater Archaeology — housed inside a Crusader castle — preserves shipwrecks and Bronze Age artifacts, offering insight into how we curate contested maritime heritage.
  • 🧭
    Ethical Travel Praxis
    Students engage as mindful observers — not passive tourists — documenting their findings and forming evidence-based responses to complex global development questions.
